About Us

Oppong Kyekyeku Company. Limited is a key distributor for Guinness Ghana Plc, based in Sekondi - Takoradi ,Western Region .

For over 20 years, we have consistently managed the supply of non-alcoholic , alcohol beverages & spirits to our cherished customers on behalf of our producer, Guinness Ghana Plc. Our dream is to see that our customers are sufficiently supplied with the needed products on a timely basis.

MISSION

Oppong Kyekyeku Co. Ltd is a Key Distributor for Gunnies Ghana Breweries Plc. We exist to provide quality and efficient delivery of both alcoholic and non – alcoholic beverages to our cherished customers upholding all best practices not comprising on quality.

VISION

To be recognized as the Leading Key Distributor of Guinness Ghana Breweries Plc high-value brands across the country and beyond with top-notch internal built structures that support and promote growth, efficiency and, innovation.
